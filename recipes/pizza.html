*If you do not have a pizza stone, you should dust whatever you are baking your pizza on (pizza pan, baking sheet) with cornmeal. This will help prevent sticking.

Ingredients and Equipment

Ingredients
  • 1 16 ounce ball of pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 3 ounces thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 5 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2.5 ounces pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese
Equipment
  • Pizza stone
  • Parchment paper
  • Pizza peel or flat baking sheet

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone in the bottom third of your oven.
  2. Preheat your oven to 550 degrees F.
  3. Prepare a work surface.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Transfer the dough to a square of parchment paper.
  6. Drizzle about a tablespoon of olive oil over the top of your dough.
  7. 1 to 2 minute par bake.
  8. Add 1 cup of pizza sauce
  9. Add 3 ounces thinly sliced mozzarella, and then top with 5 ounces of shredded mozzarella.
  10. Add about a half package of pepperoni (or olives, mushrooms, ham and pineapple, sausage, red onions, or literally any toppings you want.
  11. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese over your toppings.
  12. Bake the pizza for about 8-12 minutes.
  13. Remove your pizza from the oven using the pizza peel.
  14. Let cool a couple minutes, then transfer to a cutting board. Slice into 8 pieces and devour!
